How your data is stored & backed up. Readings live only in this browser, on this phone — nothing is sent to the cloud and nothing syncs across devices. Tapping Backup downloads one file (a .json) holding every reading; keep that file somewhere off the phone — save it to Google Drive or email it to yourself — and you’ve got a safety copy. If you ever clear browsing data or switch phones, Restore reads that file straight back in and you’re exactly where you left off. Export CSV is a different thing: a spreadsheet copy for Excel/Sheets — useful, but it can’t be loaded back in as a backup.
